PostBox
Home Goods
In close collaboration with both mail carriers and homeowners, the postbox design has evolved into a comprehensive solution, addressing concerns from both sides. Positioned as a post-mounted mailbox, it can hold up to 2 USPS medium flat-rate packages, accommodating the average daily load in the US. Importantly, it maintains weather resilience and security without compromise.

Dog Bed
Digital Concept
Designed for multi-pet households, this pet bed features two distinct layers to cater to your pets' comfort needs. The suspended mesh bottom promotes airflow, keeping your pet cool and comfortable, while the soft, plush top layer provides warmth and cozy comfort. Perfect for pets who enjoy a choice between cool and warm resting options.
Worker’s Backpack
Soft Goods
A backpack made for anyone who carries a non-traditional load. Artists, seamstresses, and woodworkers alike can handle cargo that is not rectangular, is heavy, is not flat, and more. The roll top can be curled to different lengths or left unclasped for even more versatility. The wide side pocket is stretchy enough for the thickest, tallest water bottle or umbrella. On the back, there is a hidden pocket so valuables can be safely tucked away.
